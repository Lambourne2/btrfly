We're thrilled to announce the conclusion of the inaugural season of BTRFLY 2024, our exciting Rocket League viewer tournament! Whether you're a seasoned pro or a newcomer to the game, BTRFLY 2024 delivered an exhilarating experience for all. We want to extend our heartfelt thanks to everyone who participated and supported the event. Your passion and dedication made this tournament a huge success. We can't wait to see you all again in future seasons of BTRFLY!
